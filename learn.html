About Text Formatting Elements

b define bold text

em define emphasized text

i defines italic text

strong defines important text

small defines smaller text

sub defines subscriptedtext

sup defines superscriptedtext

ins define inserted text

del define deleted text

browsers display strong as b, and em as i

About Quotation and Citation Elements

q

* q defines a short quotation

WWF's goal is to: Build a future where people live in harmony with nature.

Browsers usually insert quotation marks around q element.

blockquote

* blockquote element defines a quoted section.

Here is a quote from WWF's website:

For 50 years, WWF has been protecting the future of nature. The world's leading conservation organization, WWF works in 100 countries and is supported by 1.2 million members in the United States and close to 5 million globally.
Browsers usually indent blockquote elements

abbr

* abbr element defines an abbreviation or an acronym.

The WHO was founded in 1948.

address

* address element defines contact information of a document of article.

Written by Jon Doe.
Visit us at:
Example.com
Box 564, Disneyland
USA

cite

* cite defines the title of a work. usually displays cite in italic.

The Scream by Edward Munch. Painted in 1893.
